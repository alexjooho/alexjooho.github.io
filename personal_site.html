About me

I am from Orange County, California and I graduated from UCSD in 2021 with a degree in human biology. Prior to making the career switch, I was pre-dental, and was actually supposed to matriculate to Herman Ostrow School of Dentistry of USC this coming August. However, after much contemplation and soul searching, I decided that dentistry was not for me and that software engineering was a much better fit for me. I got introduced to this field by my friends who are studying computer science in college, and I found that I was having so much fun whenever I spent time self-learning JavaScript. As someone whose favorite subjects have always been math and physics, I have an innate love for problem solving and learning and I would love to continue to problem solve and learn in this wonderful career.

In my free time I love to spend time with my friends, girlfriend, and family. Whether it be going out for food or playing board games at home, I'm always happiest when spending time with the ones I care about. I also love to play with my dog Toby and give him treats even when I know I shouldn't.
